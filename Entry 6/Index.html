Air fryer Salmon
  • 1
    Salmon
  • 1
    Orange
  • 2tsp
    Shoyu
  • 2tsp
    Honey
  • 3tsp
    Olive Oil
  • 4 sprigs
    Salt and Pepper
Add your salmon to a bowl and add all the other ingredients. Leave it resting for a few minutes then place the marinated salmon in the air fryer and cook it for 15 minutes at 400 degrees.
Beet and Avocado Salad
  • 2
    Beets
  • 1
    Avocado
  • 1/2
    Tofu
  • 6
    Basil
  • 2tsp
    Sunflower seed
  • 1
    Lemon
  • 2tsp
    Olive Oil
  • 3 springs
    Salt and Pepper
Cook your beets without the skin until they are soft. Cut the beets, the avocado, and the tofu into squares and mix them in a bowl. Add the rest of the ingredients to your taste and enjoy it!
Pan S'mores
  • 1
    Pack of marshmallows
  • 1
    Milk chocolate bar
  • 1
    Pack of sweet crackers
Layer the chocolate on top of the cracks and add the marshmallow on top. Bake for 20 minutes at 250 degrees. Bon appetit!
