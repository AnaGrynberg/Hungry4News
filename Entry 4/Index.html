How does this relate to food design?

Well, Food Design has to do with the whole experience of eating right? And having the knowledge of which foods influence our health is crucial. A restaurant owner, for example, has to know where each ingredient was harvested and how it got to their kitchen. The owner is also responsible for the impact this food has on the customers and should design the recipes accordingly. And that my friends, is what we call Food Design!
